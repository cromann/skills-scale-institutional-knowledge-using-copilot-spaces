# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers research user needs, create design solutions, and ensure products are intuitive and accessible. They collaborate with product managers and developers to deliver exceptional user experiences.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs
- Establish and maintain design systems and patterns
- Collaborate with developers to ensure design implementation fidelity
- Advocate for accessibility and inclusive design practices

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, user-centered experiences
- Reduce friction and improve user satisfaction metrics
- Maintain design consistency across products

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- Collaboration with PM on user stories and flows
- Hand-off documentation and design specs for developers
- Research findings and usability test reports

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain the infrastructure and automation that enables rapid, reliable software delivery. They bridge development and operations to ensure systems are scalable, secure, and observable.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure as code and cloud resources
- Implement monitoring, logging, and alerting systems
- Ensure security, compliance, and disaster recovery readiness
- Support incident response and root cause analysis

### Goals
- Minimize deployment friction and cycle time
- Maximize system reliability and uptime
- Enable developer productivity through automation

### Typical Communication
- Infrastructure and deployment planning sessions
- Incident response coordination and postmortems
- Capacity planning with engineering and product teams
- Documentation of runbooks and operational procedures

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Security Leads establish and enforce security practices to protect systems, data, and users. They work across teams to identify vulnerabilities, respond to threats, and ensure compliance with security standards.

### Responsibilities
- Define security policies, standards, and best practices
- Conduct security reviews and threat modeling
- Manage vulnerability scanning and remediation
- Lead incident response for security events
- Provide security training and guidance to teams

### Goals
- Prevent security incidents and data breaches
- Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements
- Build security awareness across the organization

### Typical Communication
- Security review checkpoints in project planning
- Incident response coordination during security events
- Security advisories and vulnerability reports
- Training sessions and security awareness updates

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts transform raw data into actionable insights that inform product decisions and measure business outcomes. They collaborate with product managers and stakeholders to define metrics and evaluate success.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track key performance indicators (KPIs)
- Build dashboards and reports for stakeholders
- Analyze user behavior and product usage patterns
- Support A/B testing and experimentation
- Validate data quality and integrity

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision making
- Measure and communicate product impact
- Identify opportunities for improvement through data

### Typical Communication
- Weekly metrics reviews with product and engineering
- Ad-hoc analysis requests and findings
- Dashboard and reporting documentation
- Experimentation results and recommendations

---

## Customer Support Lead

### Role Summary
Customer Support Leads ensure customers receive timely, effective help and advocacy. They gather customer feedback, identify product issues, and work with product and engineering teams to improve the customer experience.

### Responsibilities
- Manage support ticket workflow and SLA adherence
- Escalate critical issues to engineering and product teams
- Identify trends in customer issues and feedback
- Contribute to knowledge base and self-service resources
- Coordinate with product teams on customer communications

### Goals
- Deliver excellent customer satisfaction scores
- Reduce time to resolution for customer issues
- Surface actionable product feedback to improve quality

### Typical Communication
- Daily triage of critical customer issues
- Weekly sync with product and engineering on trends
- Customer communication and status updates
- Documentation of common issues and solutions
